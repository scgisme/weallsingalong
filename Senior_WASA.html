Music can connect and unify and uplift Humans. Singing is a personal connection to that wonderful place that is Music. Singing Together allows us to feel Happier, Included, and Unified with others.


We All Sing Along is a musical, singing, activity, specifically for Elders and Dementia and Alzheimer patients.


Linda brings her guitar and voice, and plenty of songs to sing together. Big Hits from the 40's and 50's, Classic Folk tunes everybody knows, songs from great movies, hymns, seasonal/Holiday tunes, even songs from childhood, are all an opportunity, to sing along and together.


The initial Senior Sing Along visit to a facility / organization is complimentary (yep that's free!) for locations in the Gainesville FL area so let's get singing and increasing happiness for your residents! Just contact me to set it up.

Each session is from 45 minutes to an hour, variable if need be.

Please contact Linda Griffith at Linda@WeAllSingAlong.com or 352-213-7201 to learn more or schedule a visit
