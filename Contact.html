First, I want to thank you for considering my services. I feel strongly that it is my responsibility to try to bring as much health, joy and happiness to others using the musical aptitudes of which I have been blessed to those who need to see a smiling face, need to feel some inclusion and simply need to feel some love. Not all residents will be interested in attending but it is my goal to bring happiness to any that will come.

I am a Gainesville FL resident so I can offer a complimentary first sing-along session to your facility in the Gainesville area so you can assess the service I am offering at no cost. In return, I would ask for honest feedback from you (perhaps you are the Activities Director) and your residents. This feedback is very helpful in continuing to mold my sing-alongs to bring the most joy possible to your residents at a fee rate that provides great value for all parties.
